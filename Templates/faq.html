FAQs

Q. How early do I make a booking?

Booking can be made 60 days prior to pickup.

Q. Do we need a booking?

We recommend you to make a booking in advance.
Please make a booking up to 15:00 2 days before pickup through the website.

Q. How can I change or cancel the booking?

Please call or use booking inquiry form whose URL is included in the confirmation mail to contact pick up location beforehand.

Q. Can I cancel the booking just before departure?

Please call the pickup locations.
If you cancel on the date of the pickup, there will be a cancellation fee (100% of rental fee).
If you cancel to pickup due to bad weather, no fee will be charged.

Q. What is One-way service?

One-way service is available to return bikes to another location between Giant Store Imabari and Onomichi. We recommend you make a booking in advance via internet or phone call.
This service is available only in case you rent a road bike for 1 day or more.

One-way service charge: 3,000 JPY per bike

Please note that we only have a limited number of bikes.
You cannnot return a bike at Giant Store Imabari on every Tuesday due to the regular holiday.

Q. I have not got the confirmation mail after the booking via the website.

After the booking have made on the website, the confirmation mail will be sent to you automatically. If you have not got it, please check the following points.

・ The registered e-mail address is wrong.
・ Please check if the confirmation mail is in the bin or the spam folder.
・ Please make sure you can receive mails from "@giant-store.jp".
・ The mail box or server is full. If so, please remove unnecessary data.
